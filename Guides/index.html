How to Create Strong & Secure Passwords

Strong passwords protect your online accounts from hacking, data leaks, and unauthorized access. Many users reuse weak passwords without realizing the risks.

  • Use at least 12 characters
  • Combine letters, numbers, and symbols
  • Avoid names, birthdays, or common words
  • Use a different password for each website

The Password Generator on Toolify Hub creates strong passwords directly in your browser. No passwords are stored or sent to any server.
