Age Calculator

Calculate your age instantly by entering your birthdate. This tool is useful for official forms, records, and personal reference.

How the age calculation works

The age calculator compares your birthdate with today’s date and calculates the number of full years that have passed. The result updates automatically based on the current date, so the calculation remains accurate over time.

Common situations where you need to calculate age

  • Filling out government or official forms
  • School, college, or exam registrations
  • Insurance policies or employment records
  • Tracking birthdays and personal milestones

Accuracy notes

This tool calculates age in completed years. Some organizations may require age expressed in years and months or based on a specific cutoff date, so always verify the requirements before submitting official documents.

Who should use this age calculator?

This tool is suitable for students, working professionals, parents, and anyone who needs a quick and reliable way to determine age without manual calculations.

Learn more about age calculation

Age calculation rules can differ depending on institutions and countries. Our guides explain common age-related requirements, cutoff dates, and how age is calculated for official purposes.
