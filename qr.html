QR Code Generator

Create QR codes for links, text, or other information. Generated QR codes can be downloaded instantly and shared digitally or in print.

How to use this QR code generator

Enter any text or URL into the input field and click the Generate button. A QR code will appear immediately. You can scan it using a smartphone camera or a QR scanning app, then download the image if needed.

Common uses for QR codes

  • Restaurant menus and table ordering
  • Sharing website links or social media profiles
  • WiFi network sharing
  • Business cards, resumes, and portfolios
  • Event tickets, invitations, and posters

Tips for using QR codes effectively

Ensure the QR code is large enough and printed clearly for easy scanning. Always test the QR code before distributing it publicly. Avoid placing QR codes on curved or reflective surfaces, which may cause scanning issues.

Who should use this QR code generator?

This tool is useful for small business owners, students, event organizers, marketers, and anyone who needs to share information quickly using QR codes without installing additional software.
